About Me

The person behind the code.

A curious mind at the intersection of artificial intelligence, design, and human experience.

I'm G Abishek, a third-year BCA student specialising in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning at Jain University, Bangalore. My journey into tech started with a simple question: "How can software actually understand people?"

That question led me to explore backend systems, database architecture, and user interface design — not as separate disciplines, but as interconnected layers of the same human experience. I believe the best technology is invisible; it just works, elegantly.

Through my mentorship at Merkle under their Creative Director, I got a rare look into how professional product teams think — from ideation all the way to delivery. It reshaped how I approach every project: with empathy for the user first, and technical precision second.

My capstone project IntervYou — an AI-powered interview coach — was recognised at my university. It's the product of months of collaboration, iteration, and genuine care for the problem we were solving.

Outside of code, I'm an active participant in college events, a multilingual communicator (English, Tamil, Kannada, Hindi), and someone who genuinely gets excited about what's next in AI.
